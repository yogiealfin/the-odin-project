Aglio e Oglio Recipe

Spaghetti Aglio e Olio is a traditional Italian pasta dish from Naples that is easy, tasty and fast to serve.

Ingredients

  • 300g spaghetti
  • 3 cloves of garlic
  • 6tbs extra virgin olive oil
  • Fresh italian parsley
  • Red chili pepper (optional)
  • Salt

Instruction

  1. Boil a large pot of water. Once it boil add a pinch or two salt and the spaghetti to boiling water.
  2. Finely sliced garlic. You can use a garlic press to cut the garlic into tiny pieces and this also creates a creamy texture which is perfect for your spaghetti aglio olio sauce
  3. Chop the parsley into small pieces and chop chili diagonaly
  4. Heat up the frying pan on the stove using medium heat. Once it started to warm up add your olive oil, garlic, chili and the stem of your parsley cook for about 3-4 minute while keep gently mix the ingredients.
  5. Onced simmered for about 4 minute add a ladle full of pasta water
  6. Take your pasta from boiling water 1 minute less than the packed instruction and add it to the pan.
  7. Add the remaining parsley and salt for taste and keep stir it for 1 minute while increasing the stove temperature to high.
  8. Pasta is ready to serve!
