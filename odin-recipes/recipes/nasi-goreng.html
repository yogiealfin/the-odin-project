Nasi Goreng Recipe

Nasi goreng is one of indonesian National Dish. It's made with cold leftover rice, seasoned with sweet soy sauce (kecap manis) and a lot of spices.

Ingredients

  • 2 portion of leftover rice
  • 200g of chicken thigh
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 6 cloves of shallot, chopped
  • 3 spring onion, chopped
  • Sambal
  • Shrimp Paste
  • 3 Tbsp kecap manis (sweet soy sauce)
  • 3 eggs

Instruction

  1. Heat up your wok on high heat and add your chicken thigh.
  2. After your chicken almost done cooking add sambal, shrimp paste, shallot, garlic and white part of spring onion until fragrant.
  3. Add eggs to the wok, wait a bit before scrambled it
  4. After egg is half cooked add rice, green part of spring onion, kecap manis and mix it well
  5. Nasi goreng is ready to serve with sunny sideup, shrimp cracker and fried shallot!
